How "Smart rank" works

Each item gets a sort value of priority weight × 10 + score (High=30, Medium=20, Low=10, untagged=0; score 0–5). Reviewed items sink to the bottom. So a High-priority 4★ beats a Medium 5★. Use the column to re-sort however you like.
