⚡ Smart Priority Tasks

Type a task the way you'd say it. The list reads the tone — "urgent", "ASAP", "by Friday", "when I get a chance" — and sorts by inferred priority. No priority field to set.

Tip: add several lines at once — each line becomes its own task.
How priority is inferred
The text is scanned for signal words and patterns, each adding/subtracting weight:
🔴 Critical: urgent, asap, emergency, critical, now, immediately, blocker, !!, overdue
🟠 High: important, deadline, today, tomorrow, by friday, client/boss waiting, can't ship without
🔵 Medium: need to, should, this week, follow up — the default lane
🟢 Low: someday, maybe, eventually, when I get a chance, nice to have, no rush

Deadlines in the near future and ALL-CAPS / exclamation marks bump things up. Everything runs locally in your browser; tasks persist via localStorage.
